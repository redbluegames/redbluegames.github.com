Who We Are

Red Blue is a small indie studio that makes indie games for mobile platforms and, if all goes according to plan, next-gen consoles as well.

Our skills are primarily with programming for Vicious Engine, Unity3d, and Android.

Why We're Here

Some of the best memories we have are of playing games with friends or family. We hope that our games can do the same for others.

We also want to share our experiences and help other indie studios by publishing much of our code openly on sites like Github and blogging about our challenges.
