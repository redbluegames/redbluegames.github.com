Red Blue Games

Skills and Platforms

Red Blue Games is a small studio that makes indie games for mobile platforms and the web.

Our skills are primarily with programming for Vicious Engine, Android, and Unity3d.

With Unity3D we are able to publish games on all major mobile and console platforms.

Motivation

Some of the best memories we have are of playing games with friends or family. We hope that our games can do the same for others.

We also want to share our experiences and help other indie studios by publishing much of our code openly on sites like Github and blogging about our challenges.

The Team

Edward Rowe

Developer, Artist, Designer

Edward worked in the game industry for over six years after he landed a lucky internship at Vicious Cycle Software in RTP. He left to found Red Blue Games with his twin brother, Lucas, so that they could team up to make the games they loved to play together growing up.

Favorite Game: The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

Favorite Non-Video Game: Magic the Gathering

Lucas Rowe

Developer, Designer

After half a decade as a QA Engineer for web companies, Lucas decided to take the plunge into the game industry with Edward and start a game studio. Starting with a small Ninja Turtles hypercard game he made with Ed in the early 90s, Lucas has always had a passion for making games, and with the founding of Red Blue, finally gets to act on it.

Favorite Game: Earthbound
