Red Blue Games

Our Story

The game creation process at Red Blue Games is a lifetime in the making. Growing up as twin brothers, co-founders Edward and Lucas began making games to share with one another. From Mario levels drawn on spools of printer paper to Lego-based RPGs, their many experiments instilled a love of game making from an early age. Now they’ve turned this passion for making games into Red Blue Games, where they make game experiences for the rest of the world to share together.
