Mini Stackem: Action Match3

Unlike any match 3 game you've played, Mini Stackem is a fast paced, combo based, race against the clock for the highest possible score. Designed for touch, matching pieces is incredibly familiar, but the strategy is incredibly deep. Fun for beginners and experts, Mini Stackem will entertain for hours.

Features

  • Intuitive scoring
  • Real time piece matching
  • Deep combo system
  • Random color schemes
  • Leaderboards and Achievements
